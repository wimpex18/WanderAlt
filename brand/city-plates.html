notes

Each thumbnail is 80×60 in the live selector — the plates are designed so the silhouette still reads at that size: St Olaf's spire, the cathedral dome, the Doms cupola.

A national flag flies once per plate as the cultural signal: Estonia on Pikk Hermann's pole, Finland on a Senate Square embankment mast (deliberately off-church), Latvia on a riverside flagpole between Doms and Centrāltirgus.

Lime stays one element per plate as the brand signal — the Telliskivi door awning (WanderAlt's natural habitat), Helsinki's cross gleam, the Doms weathercock. Petrol is the signature mass: copper spire, copper dome, bridge cables.
